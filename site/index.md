# Have you ever had a thought? Thank a thoughtshepherd.

Let's be honest. Most of us stink at the think. If baseball
is a field of dreams, real life is a field of thoughts, and
while on the football field, we are inspired by the Gipper,
we are also led along through our pasture of perception.

The world is full of would-be thoughtshepherds – people who
want to lead us for their own nefarious purposes, or who are
just plain bad at it. Imagine taking batting advice from
[Ray Oyler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ray_Oyler). Yeah.
Don't do that.

But some thoughtshepherds are freakin' awesome. They think hard
so we don't have to! And they're great at it. Albert Einstein
famously had thoughts, and now there's a whole branch of
science named after him or something. And he's only half the
thinker of today's best thinkers.

This website is a celebration of the cream of the crop in
the field of thinkopathy. Here is where the fan clubs are.
So do you like to think? No? Then join one of these fanclubs
and let these brilliant folks do it for you.
